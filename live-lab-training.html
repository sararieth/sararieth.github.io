Overview

This Azure App Modernization live lab guide is utilized in two ways. Lab leaders use the presenter version to walk students through the material during live sessions. When lab sessions are over, students use the attendee version to review the content at their own pace.

Both versions allow the user to go through the lab as many times as they need to without spinning up their own environment, saving resource costs and reducing troubleshooting downtime.
