Microsoft Copilot Series

DIRECT LINK:Copilot experiences for insurance

Client

Microsoft for Financial Services

Project Outcome

This demo portal provides sales training modules that can also be leveraged for client product demonstrations.

Project Background

Microsoft Copilot feature development was moving fast, and the technical documentation, training, and brand assets were lagging behind. Sales teams and general users were having a difficult time keeping track of new features and how to use them effectively. We were contracted by a team of industry product owners and developers at Microsoft for Financial Services to research key features and build easy-to-navigate sales training and presentation content for industry-specific Copilot capabilities.

Collaboration and Planning Process

In collaboration with our CEO, technical program manager, and content manager I worked to determine the scripting style for the project. Individually, I researched feature capabilities covered in the module outlines as well as brand guidelines for the product family.

KEY INDIVIDUAL CONTRIBUTIONS
  • Designed portal pages for banking, insurance, and capital markets and compliance
  • Wrote and peer-edited demo scripts for four industry verticals
  • Designed demo environment content to resonate with each industry vertical (e.g. fictitious user emails, Copilot interactions, persona interactions)
  • Generated AI audio voiceover
  • Finalized production of interactive content for four industry verticals
TOOLS

Photoshop, Audacity, Microsoft 365, Copilot, ElevenLabs, DemoMate (in-house demo creation software)

STYLE GUIDES AND LEGAL RESOURCES

Microsoft Writing Style Guide, Microsoft Brand Central, Microsoft CELA (Corporate, External, and Legal Affairs) documentation
