Background on the Demo Shown in this Example

A team of industry product owners and developers at Microsoft Industry Solutions reached out with a request to build industry-specific, day-in-the-life product demos, featuring Microsoft 365 Copilot and AI agents, with the goal of helping their sales teams learn about and present new features.

Before

View the before

At the demo kick-off, our technical manager finalized the demo flow with the client, captured a rough set of screens to work with, and provided me with the initial screen input linked here along with a few notes to go on. In the link provided, those notes can be viewed in the reading pane on the right side of the screen.

Once this input was received, I picked up the project and owned it to completion.

After

View the final product

As the sole writer, designer, and producer, here's what I contributed, and the process I followed in order to bring the final product to life in two weeks’ time:

  • Researched feature capabilities, real-world use case scenarios, and Microsoft's current brand positioning
  • Developed personas and crafted storylines
  • End-to-end production
    • Wrote demo scripts
    • Designed demo environment content to resonate with each industry vertical (e.g. fictitious user emails, Copilot interactions, persona interactions)
    • Generated AI audio voiceover
    • Produced interactive content (e.g. image editing and cleanup, audio pacing, adding interactive user prompts)
